System_Specs

KEFE QR_ENGINE utilizes high-precision Reed-Solomon error correction. The output is generated as a raw SVG (Scalable Vector Graphics) path. Unlike raster formats (PNG/JPG), SVG ensures 100% scan accuracy at any magnification level.

Procedural_Note

All encoding is handled client-side via browser memory. No data is transmitted to external servers. High-density data strings may automatically increase the QR version (Matrix size) to maintain integrity.
